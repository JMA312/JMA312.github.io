Social Media App for University Students

Bind is a social media app designed for university students. The prototype was built to make it easier for students to form meaningful connections with one another, especially during the pandemic.

The app would have messaging between students, formation of interest and study groups, the ability to add classes/meetups to your calendar, etc. Pictures down below!
